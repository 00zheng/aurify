        How To Play

        1. Start by clicking Harvest Credits for your first cash.
        2. Buy Factory upgrades in this order: Droppers, Conveyor, Collector, Multiplier.
        3. Upgrade Plot Tier to unlock higher production caps and stronger scaling.
        4. Use building tools to expand your city and increase city-based income.
        5. Complete tasks and claim rewards to jump into mid-game faster.
        6. Reinvest constantly for compounding growth. Credits never go below 0.

        Core tycoon loop: passive income -> reinvestment -> faster production -> bigger unlocks.
