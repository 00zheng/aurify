      How To Play

      1. Build Money Droppers and Fusion Cores directly on the map using the Build Palette.
      2. Add Conveyors and Battery Relays to speed production and support power demand.
      3. Stand on a Dropper, Core, Conveyor, or Battery Relay and press E to upgrade it.
      4. Click Harvest Credits or stand on a Fusion Core and press E for active boosts.
      5. Complete tasks and claim rewards to accelerate progression.
      6. Reinvest constantly for compounding growth. Credits never go below 0.

      Core tycoon loop: passive income -> reinvestment -> faster production -> bigger unlocks.
